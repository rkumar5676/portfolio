Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard
A Power BI dashboard analyzing customer churn patterns, identifying high-risk segments, and tracking retention metrics across demographics, contract types, and service usage — critical for revenue protection strategy.
💡 About this Project: Built with Power BI Desktop using telecom customer dataset. Analyzes churn by contract type, tenure, payment method, and service features. Includes cohort analysis, churn trend by month, and risk-segment breakdown with DAX measures.
🐈 View on GitHub 🏠 Visit Portfolio

🎯 Key Business Insights Delivered

🚫 High-Risk Segment Identified
Month-to-month contract customers with fiber optic service churn at 42% — highest risk group
📈 Tenure Impact Analysis
Customers in first 12 months are 3x more likely to churn — early retention strategy recommended
💰 Revenue Protection Model
Quantified ₹16.1M revenue at risk with priority customer list for retention campaign targeting
✅ Retention Recommendation
Annual contract conversion for high-risk customers projected to reduce churn rate by 15%
